Introduction

Insane Almighty Heights is an African-American town gang located on Calton Heights, San Fierro. The Insane Almighty Heights is one of known Bloods Gang in San Fierro. New Generation Since the new generation of Insane Almighty Heights, they're claim Chinatown. Chinatown has been abandoned due to the former war between gangs that happened 2 years ago. Insane Almighty Heights commonly posted on Calton Heights as their main set.
